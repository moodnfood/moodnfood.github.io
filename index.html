Our Story

Passion on a Plate,
Story in Every Bite

Founded in 2014 by Chef Asad Mirza, Mood&Food began as a humble bistro with a single conviction — that exceptional food can transform an ordinary evening into an extraordinary memory.

Today, we are proud to serve over 500 guests nightly across our two-floor dining space, blending contemporary global cuisine with the rich culinary heritage of Pakistan.
