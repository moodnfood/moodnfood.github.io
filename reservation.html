Frequently Asked Questions

Can I walk in without a reservation? ＋

Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability. However, we strongly recommend making a reservation, especially for evenings and weekends, as tables fill up quickly. Reservations guarantee your preferred seating.

Is there parking available? ＋

Yes, complimentary valet parking is available at the entrance. We also have a dedicated car park with 60 spaces on the basement level. Street parking is available on Sunset Boulevard.

Do you cater for private events and parties? ＋

Absolutely. We have two private dining rooms accommodating 12 and 30 guests respectively. We offer bespoke menus, custom decorations, and dedicated event coordinators. Please email events@moodfood.pk for enquiries.

What is your dress code? ＋

We maintain a smart casual dress code. We kindly ask that guests avoid beachwear, sportswear, and flip-flops. Business casual and formal attire is always welcome and appreciated.

Are children welcome? ＋

Yes, Mood&Food is a family-friendly restaurant. We have a dedicated children's menu and high chairs are available upon request. The ground floor is fully accessible for prams and wheelchairs.
