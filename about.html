How It All Began

From a Small Bistro to Karachi's Finest Table

In the winter of 2014, Chef Asad Mirza left behind a thriving career at a five-star hotel in Dubai with a single, audacious dream: to bring world-class fine dining to the heart of Karachi. Armed with 12 years of culinary mastery and an unwavering belief in the transformative power of great food, he opened Mood&Food in a modest two-room space on Sunset Boulevard.

The first week was quiet. The second week, a food columnist stopped by on a whim and published a glowing review the following Sunday. By the third week, there was a queue at the door. Within six months, Mood&Food had been awarded "Best New Restaurant" by the Pakistan Hospitality Awards — and the rest, as they say, is delicious history.

What began with 20 seats and a team of four has grown into a two-floor dining destination welcoming over 500 guests each evening, a team of 65 dedicated professionals, and a menu that changes with every season to honour the finest ingredients Pakistan and the world have to offer.

The People Behind the Plates

Meet Our Culinary Team

Passionate professionals who transform quality ingredients into extraordinary experiences every single day.

Chef Asad Mirza

Founder & Executive Chef

With 18 years of culinary experience spanning Dubai, London, and Paris, Asad founded Mood&Food with the dream of redefining Pakistani fine dining. His philosophy: simplicity elevated to perfection.

Chef Zara Siddiqui

Head Pastry Chef

A graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Zara brings French patisserie mastery to Mood&Food's dessert menu. Her chocolate fondant alone has earned the restaurant three separate awards.

Omar Farooq

Restaurant Manager

Omar ensures that every guest's experience is flawless from arrival to departure. With a background in luxury hospitality, he leads the front-of-house team with precision and warmth.

Chef Imran Ali

Sous Chef — Mains

Imran's expertise in meat cookery and sauce-making forms the backbone of Mood&Food's main course repertoire. Trained under a Michelin-starred mentor in Istanbul, his technique is extraordinary.
